Academic R&D Systems Engineer & Team Member

SAE Aero Design - Unmanned Aerial Vehicle

An award-winning academic project focused on designing, building, and competing with a custom UAV. This experience formed the foundation of my system integration, problem-solving, and technical documentation skills.

The Challenge: Building a High-Performance UAV from Scratch

The SAE Aero Design competition is a prestigious international challenge where student teams design and build a radio-controlled aircraft to meet a series of demanding mission requirements. Our goal was to create a highly efficient, lightweight UAV capable of maximizing its payload capacity while adhering to strict design constraints.

My Role & Contributions

As a key member of the Poznań University of Technology team, I was involved in multiple aspects of the project, including:

  • **System Integration:** Ensuring all subsystems (aerodynamics, structure, propulsion, electronics) worked together as a cohesive unit.
  • **Structural Design & Analysis:** Contributing to the design of the airframe to balance strength and weight.
  • **Prototyping & Manufacturing:** Hands-on work building the aircraft using composite materials.
  • Technical Documentation & Thesis: As the lead author of the technical report, I documented our entire design process. This report not only won 1st place in its category but also formed the basis of my successful Bachelor's Thesis defense, as mentioned on the official team website.
