Architecting Resilient Systems for Mission-Critical Applications

As a Systems Integration & Embedded Architect, I specialize in transforming complex requirements into reliable, mission-critical hardware and software. With a background rooted in military vehicle modernization and industrial R&D, I have a deep understanding of designing resilient, "soldier-proof" systems.

My philosophy is simple: architecture first. I design for scalability, reliability, and the end-user – whether it's a pilot in a simulator or an operator on a production line. This portfolio is a curated selection of projects that demonstrate this approach.
